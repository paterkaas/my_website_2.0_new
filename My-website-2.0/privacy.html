Privacy Policy

Transparency & Trust

How we handle your data

At TravelCompAInion, we take your privacy seriously. Since we use AI and affiliate partners, we want to be transparent about what data we collect and how we use it.

1. General

TravelCompAInion is a free-to-use travel guide. We do not ask for personal account details or registration to use the website.

2. AI Usage (Google Gemini)

When you use our chat feature ("Brabant Insider AI"), your message is sent to the Google Gemini API to generate a response.

  • Do not share sensitive personal information (such as last names, addresses, or payment details) in the chat.
  • The content of the conversations is processed by Google to generate answers but not permanently stored by us.

3. Affiliate Links

Our website contains links to partners such as Booking.com.

  • If you click through our link and make a booking, we may receive a small commission.
  • This costs you nothing extra, but helps us keep the website running.

4. Contact

Do you have questions regarding this policy? Please contact us via the contact page.
